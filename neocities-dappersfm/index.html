Why Do I Like These?

I Think I Left the Stove On by Hotel Ugly just keeps playing over and over again in my head like a broken record, specificaly that lead guitar. I really enjoy the sound and the melody of it

Telephones by Vacations is just so calming, starting with a slow guitar melody backed by a telephone being dialed, marking the drums begining. These elements combined with the lead singer(Campbell Burns)'s soft vocals makes me so sleepy in a good way.

GONE GONE / THANK YOU by Tyler, The Creator is a 2 for 1 song deal, so lets go in order. GONE GONE has an imaculate beat and melody to it. Tyler's singing voice soothes me the whole song. THANK YOU has a differnt feeling to it, still bangs though. However the main catch to why I like it is that "HA" sound with the beat.
