Why Do I Like These?

Monster Song (Demo) by Dom Fera: This song has been stuck in my head for like months since I first heard it, and it's just so fun to sing along to. This song is so fun to sing along to in fact, that I sang along to it 5 times while I was writing this (I have the song on repeat, gotta take in as much of the song as possible). Dom Fera's vocal performance is just so fucking good in all his songs, but since I had to choose only one, I chose Monster Song (Demo) to be the top candidate (sorry Northern Star).

Mr. Incorrect by Malcom Todd is quite the slow and chill beat, with Todd's smooth and kinda whispery voice just cooly floating above it. Malcom;s songs are also something I love to sing along with (starting to think this is a low bar for my favorite songs... hnn), and may also be easier to sing along to since its not like he's belting the entire song. The choosing one specific Malcom Todd song for this update's selection, but this one ultimately won against Earrings, and Chest Pain (I Love).

Rotary by Archer Oh is something you guys might be a little more familiar with me listeniong to because guess fucking WHAT??????? Its indie rooooock. Specifically a Latino one, just like Late Night Drive Home (who recently released a new album!). However, unlike Late Night Drive Home, Archer Oh (at least for this song) goes a little bit harder with the vocals, with quite a lot of yelling. I was actually introduced to this band through this song randomly playing at 12am, and I can't think of a better song to introduce me to them (and Easy Eyes is going to be in the next selection).
