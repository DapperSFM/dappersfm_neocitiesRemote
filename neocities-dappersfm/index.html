Why Do I Like These?

Song for Me by Greer is still fairly new to me and what introduced me to Greer in the first place, but god is it good. Lead singer Josiah's vocals are amazing, calming with a twang to it I can't quite place. Indie Rock never fails to find the best instruments.

The Basement by Lunar Vacation is, shocker, another rock song. This one's Surf Rock though. Grace Repasky does an amazing job as lead singer and the effects added to her voice in this song really just add to it.

Rude Mountain by Penny Parker... Oh Penny Parker, the woman that you are. I love her vocals on this song and I can't help but to sing along, especially at the pre-/actual chorus (like you do on all songs I guess but-). Penny is so FUCKING GOALS AAAAAAAA!

Don't forget to check back every once in a while to see new songs in the rotation!
